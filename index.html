Frequently Asked Questions

1. How quickly can you deliver a dumpster?

Our standard rental period typically spans seven to ten days, which provides ample time for most residential and commercial projects. If your project requires more time, we offer flexible extensions for a small daily fee. Simply contact our office before your scheduled pickup date to adjust your timeline.Most orders placed by 2 PM local time are delivered next business day. Emergency requests (storm cleanup, urgent projects) receive priority routing.

2. What items are prohibited?

Hazardous materials (paint, chemicals, tires, batteries) cannot be accepted. We provide a clear disposal guide upfront and partner with local recyclers for restricted items where possible.

3. Do I need a permit for street placement?

It depends on your municipality. We’ll advise during booking and handle permit coordination in cities like Chicago, Boston, or San Francisco where required.

4. How do I choose the right size?

Tell us your project type (e.g., “basement cleanout,” “roofing tear-off”), and we’ll recommend the optimal container. No guesswork—just practical guidance.

5. Are there hidden fees?

Never. Your quote includes delivery, pickup, disposal, and applicable taxes. Overage fees only apply if weight exceeds agreed limits (we’ll clarify thresholds upfront)
